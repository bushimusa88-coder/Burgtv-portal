📱 How to find MAC Address

  • Fire TV: Settings → My Fire TV → About → Network

  • LG TV: Settings → All Settings → General → About This TV
  • Apple TV: Settings → General → About
  • Mobile: Auto-generated in app

📖 Setup Guide

  1. Download the app on your device (TV, Fire Stick, Mobile)
  2. Open the app and find the MAC Address displayed

  4. Enter the MAC Address and your M3U playlist URL
